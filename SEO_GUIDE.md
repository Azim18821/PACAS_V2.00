## ‚úÖ What I Just Added

### 1. **Meta Tags** (in index.html)
- Title, description, keywords

### 2. **robots.txt** - Tells search engines what to crawl
- Allows all pages except /admin and /api
- Points to sitemap

### 3. **sitemap.xml** - Helps Google index your pages
- Lists all important pages
- Shows update frequency
- Sets priority levels

### Step 1: Google Search Console (FREE - Most Important!)
1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Sign in with Google account
3. Click "Add Property" ‚Üí Enter `https://pacashomes.co.uk`
4. Verify ownership (choose HTML tag or DNS method)
5. Submit your sitemap: `https://pacashomes.co.uk/sitemap.xml`

**This tells Google your site exists and to index it!**
